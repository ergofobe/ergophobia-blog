A small week, all of it the plumbing that has to exist before a game can carry
ads responsibly.

I put the project formally under its corporate owner — proper attribution and a
real contact address rather than something ad hoc — and published a privacy
policy reachable from the hub. With those in place I worked through the
onboarding an ad network requires so the surface is ready to switch on later,
without scrambling on the compliance pieces at the last minute.

Nothing here changes how the puzzle plays. It's the kind of week that doesn't
show up on screen, but it clears the path for the part that does.
