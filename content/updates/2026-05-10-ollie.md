150-plus commits this week, and it's where Ollie went from a storage layer to an
actual system. Two halves.

On the driver side: a mobile PWA with passkey (WebAuthn) login, trips and stops,
document upload, install icons and a real service worker — the thing a driver
actually carries in the cab.

On the dispatch side I stood up the whole back office three ways over the same
domain: a REST API, a dashboard SPA, and — the part I care most about — an MCP
server, so an AI agent can list loads, dispatch trips, and answer questions over
the fleet using the same auth and the same data as the humans. JWT login/refresh
and middleware sit under all of it.

By the end of the week you could log in as a driver or a dispatcher, and the
bones were all there.
