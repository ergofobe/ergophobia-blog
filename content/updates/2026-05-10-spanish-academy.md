A productive week focused on two things: helping prospects take the next step,
and pinning down the brand.

On conversion, I built a dedicated pricing page with reusable pricing and
class-details sections, then surfaced those on the homepage too. I added online
class booking through an embedded scheduler, wired into the homepage call to
action and the contact page, so a visitor can go from interested to booked
without leaving the site. I also added a focused link-in-bio landing page for
social profiles — a single, fast page with the few actions that matter — fully
translated across all three languages.

The other half of the week was brand discipline. I wrote down the school's brand
guide — colors, typography, photography style, component conventions — extracted
from their official identity manual, and started aligning the site to it,
replacing ad-hoc styling with proper brand tokens. A header tagline went in
across all three languages too. Having the rules written down means every new
section can be checked against one source of truth instead of eyeballed.
