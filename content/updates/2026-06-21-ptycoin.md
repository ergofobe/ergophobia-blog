This week leaned into reader experience and measurement.

I added a "Keep reading" related-posts shelf that scores candidates by topic and
a tiered shelf-life, so evergreen guides stay surfaced while dated market reads
age out. I also built an all-time weekly chart variant with long-run moving
averages for the bigger-picture markets analysis pieces.

On internationalization, I finished the language-preference work: an inline
redirect in the page head that respects a returning reader's choice, plus
persistence when they flip the switcher, so people stop landing on the wrong
language. To know whether any of this helps, I instrumented the language redirect
and the related-posts clicks and added a small analytics-review workflow to read
engagement and language fit back out.

Process-wise I moved all non-content development behind worktrees and PRs rather
than committing straight to main, and cleaned up pagination styling on list
pages. Underneath all of it, the daily news, markets, policy and guide cadence
kept running, bilingually.
