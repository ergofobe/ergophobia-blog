## Overview

An automated pipeline that gathers and curates high-conviction signals — crypto,
tech, liberty, and ethics — into a daily report published here. The goal is
signal without the feed: no memes, no ragebait, just the threads worth keeping,
each tied back to its source.

## How it works

The pipeline pulls from X, web sources and communities, filters for
high-conviction material over noise, clusters it by theme, and writes it up as a
dated digest with quotes and links. Still a work in progress — the curation and
scheduling are where most of the iteration is happening.

**Stack:** automation · LLM curation.
